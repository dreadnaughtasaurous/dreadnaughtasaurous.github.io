# 54. Telephone Allowance

When the Health Service requires a Doctor to be in telephone contact for work purposes, the Health Service must provide a fully funded mobile phone for the Doctor’s work use; or fully reimburse the Doctor for all reasonable and actual costs (that is maintenance and rental) incurred by the Doctor when making or receiving work-related telephone calls.

Plain language terms: phone allowance.
