# 65. Prenatal Leave

**65.1** A Doctor required to attend pre-natal appointments or parenting classes that are only available or can only be attended during the Doctor’s ordinary rostered shift may, subject to provision of satisfactory evidence of attendance, access his or her personal leave credit.

**65.2** The Doctor must give the Health Service prior notice of the Doctor’s intention to take such leave.

Plain language terms: antenatal, pregnancy appointments.
