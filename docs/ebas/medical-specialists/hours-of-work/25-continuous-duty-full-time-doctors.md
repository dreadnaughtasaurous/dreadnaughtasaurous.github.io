# 25. Continuous Duty (Full-Time Doctors)

All full time Doctors will remain on duty when patient needs require, notwithstanding the occurrence of normal meal breaks, conferences or the expiration of their normal hours. Payment for this availability is included in the rates of pay for Full-time Doctors in Part 1 of [Appendix 2](/ebas/medical-specialists/appendices/2-specialists-remuneration-and-allowances).
