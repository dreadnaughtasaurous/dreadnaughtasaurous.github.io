# 43. Provision of Mobile Phone or Reimbursement of Cost

When a Health Service requires a Doctor to be in telephone contact for work purposes, the Health Service must provide a fully funded mobile phone for the Doctor’s work use; or fully reimburse the Doctor for all reasonable and actual costs incurred by the Doctor when making or receiving work related telephone calls.
