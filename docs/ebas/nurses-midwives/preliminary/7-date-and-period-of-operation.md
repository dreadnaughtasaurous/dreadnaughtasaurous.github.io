# 7. Date And Period Of Operation

**7.1** This Agreement will operate seven days after the date upon which it is approved by the Commission.

**7.2** The Nominal Expiry Date of this Agreement is 30 April 2028. The Agreement will continue in force after the expiry date until replaced by a further enterprise agreement.
