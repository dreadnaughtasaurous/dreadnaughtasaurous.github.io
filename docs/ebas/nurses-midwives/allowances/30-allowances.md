# 30. Allowances



**30.1** The rates for allowances are set out in [Appendix 2](/ebas/nurses-midwives/appendices/02-wages-and-allowances).

Note: Excepting vehicle, uniform, laundry, change of shift and meal allowances, allowances are calculated to the nearest 10 cents with any amount of 5 cents being rounded up.

Change of shift allowances payable pursuant to [clause 41](/ebas/nurses-midwives/allowances/41-change-of-shift-allowance-preservation-ens-only) are calculated to the nearest 5 cents, portions of a cent being disregarded.
