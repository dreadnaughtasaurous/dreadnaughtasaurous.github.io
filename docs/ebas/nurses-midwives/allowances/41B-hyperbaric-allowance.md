# 41B. Hyperbaric Allowance

**41B.1** From 1 June 2024 an Employee working in, or on call for, a hyperbaric chamber shall be paid the additional allowance at [Appendix 2](/ebas/nurses-midwives/appendices/02-wages-and-allowances):

- **(a)** For each shift in which the Employee undertakes a pressurisation/dive; and
- **(b)** In addition to the on-call allowance, for each period of on call where the Employee must comply with restrictions because, in the event of recall, they may be required to undertake a pressurisation/dive.

**41B.2** This allowance is not payable during periods of leave.
