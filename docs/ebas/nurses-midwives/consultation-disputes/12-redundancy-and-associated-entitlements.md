**12.2 Definitions**

- **(a)** **Affected Employee** means an Employee whose role will be redundant.
- **(b)** **Comparable Role** means an ongoing role that:
  - **(i)** is the same occupation (or acceptable to the Employee);
  - **(ii)** is in the same or an acceptable clinical specialty, or one they could undertake with reasonable support;
  - **(iii)** is at the same Level (salary);
  - **(iv)** accounts for normal ordinary hours worked;
  - **(v)** is a **Reasonable Distance** from current work;
  - **(vi)** takes personal/family circumstances into account; and
  - **(vii)** considers health and safety.
- **(c)** **Consultation** is as defined at [clause 11](/ebas/nurses-midwives/consultation-disputes/11-consultation).
- **(d)** **Continuity of Service** means service is treated as unbroken for personal leave transfers.
- **(e)** **Level** means a weekly salary at least equal to or higher than the previous role.
- **(f)** **Reasonable Distance** considers original work location, home address, travel capacity, time, and family commitments.
- **(g)** **Redeployment period** means 13 weeks from the date the Employer notifies the Employee that consultation is complete.
- **(h)** **Redundancy** occurs when the Employer no longer requires the job to be performed by anyone due to operational changes.
- **(i)** **Relocation** means moving to a different Campus (temporary or permanent).
- **(j)** **Salary maintenance** is the difference between the old and new pay rates following redeployment.

**12.3 Redeployment**

- **(a)** Affected Employees will be considered for redeployment during the 13-week period.
- **(b)** Employees must be advised in writing of the redundancy date, process, support, and rights.
- **(c)** **Employer obligations**: Make every effort to find a Comparable Role, provide a case manager, and give priority to Affected Employees for new roles (e.g., 'spill and fill').
- **(d)** **Employee obligations**: Participate in the process, identify training needs, and develop a resume.
- **(e)** **Rejecting a Comparable Role**: May make the Employee ineligible for a departure/severance package.
- **(f)** **Temporary duties**: Employees may be transferred to temporary duties within their skills/profession while awaiting redeployment.
- **(g)** **Support for redeployment**: Includes relevant theory training, up to 12 weeks of supernumerary work, and educator support.
- **(h)** **No redeployment available**: If redeployment is unlikely, the Employee can accept redundancy with an additional payment of the lesser of 13 weeks or the remaining redeployment period.

**12.4 Support to Affected Employees**

Support includes counselling, retraining, application/interview coaching, time off for interviews, and funding for independent financial advice.

**12.5 Salary Maintenance**

- **(a)** Entitlement applies if the new role has lower pay, fewer hours, or reduced eligibility for penalties/loadings.
- **(b)** Maintenance lasts for **52 weeks** unless the Employee accepts another position with equal or higher pay.
- **(c)** Accrued long service and annual leave are preserved at their pre-redeployment value; personal leave is preserved in hours.

**12.6 Relocation**

- **(a)** Employers must provide at least 7 days' written notice of relocation. It must be a Reasonable Distance, and information on the new site's amenities must be provided.
- **(b)** **Relocation Allowance**: If relocation causes extra costs, the Employee can apply for an allowance.
- **(c)** **Payment**: A lump sum up to **$1,900.00** based on a written estimate of additional travel/expenses for up to 12 months.
- **(d)** **Exceptions**: Allowance is not paid if the new site is a location the Employee could normally be deployed to under existing conditions.

**12.7 Termination due to Redundancy**

Entitlements are governed by the *Public Sector Industrial Relations Policies 2015*.

**12.8 Exception to Severance Pay**

Severance pay does not apply if the Employer secures a Comparable Role with another Employer covered by this Agreement that:
- Is within a Reasonable Distance;
- Provides Continuity of Service; and
- Applies salary maintenance if there is a loss of income.

Plain language terms: redundancy pay, retrenchment, position made redundant.
