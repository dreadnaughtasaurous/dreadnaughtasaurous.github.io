# 86. Skill Mix

- **(a)** The minimum skill mix that each Employer in respect of the hospitals specified in Schedule 2 of the *Safe Patient Care Act* aims to achieve in all acute/general surgical and medical wards is:
  - **(i)** 1/3 Registered Nurse with more than three years' experience;
  - **(ii)** 1/3 Registered Nurse with one to three years' experience; and
  - **(iii)** 1/3 Registered Nurse with graduate nurse/Enrolled Nurse.
- **(b)** From 1 January 2025, the minimum skill mix that each Employer aims to achieve in all operating theatres and paediatric wards is:
  - **(i)** 1/3 Registered Nurse with more than three years' experience;
  - **(ii)** 1/3 Registered Nurse with one to three years' experience; and
  - **(iii)** 1/3 graduate Registered Nurse/Enrolled Nurse.

Plain language terms: skill mix, ratios, nurse mix.
