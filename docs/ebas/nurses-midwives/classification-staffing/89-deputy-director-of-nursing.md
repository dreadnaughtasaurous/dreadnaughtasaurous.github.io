# 89. Deputy Director Of Nursing

During the life of this Agreement, Employers which operate a hospital of over 30 beds across more than one site or Campus may give consideration to the appointment of a Deputy Director of Nursing on each Campus.

Plain language terms: DDoN, deputy DON.
