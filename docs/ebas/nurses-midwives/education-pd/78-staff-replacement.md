# 78. Staff Replacement

All absences resulting from approved leave under [clauses 75](/ebas/nurses-midwives/education-pd/75-professional-development-leave) (professional development leave), 76 (study leave) and 77 (examination leave) will be back-filled in clinical areas where that Employee would ordinarily have a patient allocation.
