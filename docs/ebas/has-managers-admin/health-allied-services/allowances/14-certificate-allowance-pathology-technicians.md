# 14. Certificate Allowance Pathology Technicians

**14.1** Employees who have successfully completed a course entitled Certificate for Mortuary Technician conducted by RMIT, or its equivalent, will be paid a weekly a Certificate Allowance – Pathology Technician pursuant to **Part 1** of [Schedule 2C](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/2c-allowances-health-allied-services).
