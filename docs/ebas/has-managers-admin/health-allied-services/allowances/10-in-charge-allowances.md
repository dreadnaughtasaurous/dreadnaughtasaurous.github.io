# 10. In Charge Allowances

**10.1** An Employee will be paid an In-charge Allowance where they are appointed or delegated to exercise control over other Employees, as follows:

| Extent of Control | % Allowance |
| --- | --- |
| In charge of 1 to 9 other Employees | 7% |
| In charge of 10 to 29 other Employees | 10% |
| In charge of 30 or more Employees | 15% |

**10.4** The In-Charge Allowance will be additional to any other allowance to which the Employee is entitled under the Agreement.

Plain language terms: in-charge, leading hand.
