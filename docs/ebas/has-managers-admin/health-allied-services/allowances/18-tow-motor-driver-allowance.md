# 18. Tow Motor Driver Allowance



Health and Allied Services Employees, other than those classified as Driver, operating a tow motor or tow loader or similar vehicle or a fork lift for a minimum of two hours in any one day will be paid a Tow Motor Driver Allowance pursuant to Part 1 of [Schedule 2C](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/2c-allowances-health-allied-services).

Plain language terms: forklift allowance.
