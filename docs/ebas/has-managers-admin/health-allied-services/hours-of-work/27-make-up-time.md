# 27. Make Up Time

**27.1** An Employee may, with the agreement of the Employer, work make up time under which the Employee takes time off during ordinary hours and works those hours at ordinary time rate at a later, mutually agreed time or times.

**27.2** Any agreement on make up time will be in writing and retained on the Employee’s personal file.

Plain language terms: time off in lieu.
