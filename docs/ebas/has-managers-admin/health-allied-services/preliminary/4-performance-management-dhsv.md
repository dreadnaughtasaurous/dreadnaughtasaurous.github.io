# 4. Performance Management (DHSV)

*This clause only applies to Dental Assistants employed by DHSV.*

**4.1** The Employer applies a Performance Management Process in respect of full time and regular part-time Employees.

**4.2** The Employer and Employees are committed to improved productivity, workplace skills, and effectiveness and Employee motivation. To this end, Dental Assistants employed under this Agreement agree to participate in the Employer’s performance management system. This includes, but is not limited to, the use of work performance plans.

The terms and conditions of employment associated with Part B – Consultation, Dispute Resolution and Discipline for Employees classified under this Section 2 - Health & Allied Services Employees and Dental Assistants are found in Section 1 – Common Terms
