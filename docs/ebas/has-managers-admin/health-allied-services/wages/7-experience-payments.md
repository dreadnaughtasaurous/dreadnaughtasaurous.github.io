# 7. Experience Payments

*This clause only applies to Employees other than Dental Assistants employed by DHSV* 

The experience payments in [Schedule 2C](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/2c-allowances-health-allied-services) as it applies to Health and Allied Services Employees will be added to the hourly ordinary time rates of pay and paid for all purposes for all Employees who have completed a year of experience or greater within the respective Wage Skill Group.
