Structure

**37.1** Food & Domestic Services Assistants

**(a)** Translation From FFPPOA 1 July 2021, Employees classified as Food & Domestic Services Assistants will translate to the new Food & Domestic Services classification structure as follows:

Current classification New classification Food and Domestic Services Assistant Food and Domestic Services Assistant (less than 3 months) Grade 1 Food and Domestic Services Assistant Food and Domestic Services Assistant (more than 3 months) Grade 2
