# 42. Dental Assistants Working In Mobile Dental Programs

**42.1** Where a Dental Assistant is required by their Employer to work off-site as part of a mobile dental program (e.g. Smile Squad), the Dental Assistant:

- **(a)** will be paid no less than if the work was performed on-site for the Employer;
- **(b)** will be paid for the travel time to and from their substantive workplace and the program site;
- **(c)** will not be disadvantaged with respect to the accrual of leave or the accrual of service (including Long Service Leave continuity and Personal Leave portability)

**42.2** For the purpose of this clause, off-site means mobile unit/vehicle or external premises, including schools or other community centres that aren’t premises of the Employer.
