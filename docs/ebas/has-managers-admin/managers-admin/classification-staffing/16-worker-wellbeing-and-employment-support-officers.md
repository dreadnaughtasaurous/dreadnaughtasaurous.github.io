# 16. Worker Wellbeing And Employment Support Officers

**16.1** From commencement of this Agreement, the role of:

- **(a)** Worker Wellbeing Officers will be introduced in accordance with Schedule 3F;
- **(b)** Aboriginal Employment Support Officers will be introduced in accordance with [Schedule 3G](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/3g-aboriginal-employment-support-officer);
- **(c)** Disability Employment Support Officers will be introduced in accordance with [Schedule 3H](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/3h-disability-employment-support-officer); and
- **(d)** Veteran Employment Support Officers will be introduced in accordance with Employees who perform the roles listed in [subclause 16.1](/ebas/has-managers-admin/managers-admin/classification-staffing/16-worker-wellbeing-and-employment-support-officers) will be classified under as Grade 3 under Part 1 or Part 2 of [Schedule 3D](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/3d-classification-structure-managers-and-administrative-workers) (as applicable).

Plain language terms: wellbeing officer, support officer.
