# 9. Weekend Work

**9.1** All rostered time of ordinary duty performed between midnight on Friday and Midnight on Sunday will be paid for at the rate of 150% (based on 1/38th of the weekly salary set out in [Schedule 3B](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/3b-wage-rates-managers-and-administrative-workers))

**9.2** Where Employees are required to carry out duties on a Saturday or Sunday in excess of the weeks work, such duties will be paid for at the rate of 200% (based on 1/38ths of the weekly salary set out in [Schedule 3B](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/3b-wage-rates-managers-and-administrative-workers)).

Plain language terms: weekend penalty, weekend loading, Saturday rate, Sunday rate.
