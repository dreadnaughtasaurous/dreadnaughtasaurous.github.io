# 2. Application Of Section 3

**2.1** The terms of Section 3 of this Agreement are additional terms specific to Managers and Administrative Workers (as defined in [Schedule 3D](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/3d-classification-structure-managers-and-administrative-workers)).

**2.2** For the avoidance of doubt, all entitlements and terms contained in Section 3 of this Agreement have no application to Health and Allied Services Employees or Dental Assistants (as defined in Section 2).

**2.3** References to clause numbers in this section refer to the applicable clause within this Section.
