# 15. Working With Children Check

**15.1 Requirement to obtain and maintain Working with Children Check** 

Where an Employee is required to possess a Working with Children Check in accordance with the Working with Children Act 2005 (or its successor), it is a condition of the Employee’s employment that they are required to obtain and maintain their own Working with Children Check.

**15.2 Dispute** 

In the event of a dispute as to whether an Employee is required to possess a Working with Children Check in accordance with the Working with Children Act 2005 (or its successor), it will be resolved in accordance with [clause 17](/ebas/has-managers-admin/common-terms/consultation-disputes/17-dispute-resolution-procedure) (Dispute Resolution Procedure).

Plain language terms: WWCC, working with children.
