# 12. Multi Cultural Awareness

**12.1** Victoria is a diverse and multicultural community, which is reflected in the patients and employees of health services.

**12.2** The parties are committed to a workplace and society free from cultural insensitivity.

**12.3** To this end, to prevent cultural misunderstandings and foster more harmonious workplaces that are sensitive to the needs of our diverse community, the parties agree to increased awareness of Employees in cultural customs and cross-cultural communication.

Plain language terms: cultural awareness, diversity.
