# 7. Commencement Date And Period Of Operation



**7.1** This Agreement will come into effect seven days after the date of approval by the Commission.

**7.2** This Agreement will nominally expire on 30 June 2025.

**7.3** The Agreement will continue to operate after the nominal expiry date in accordance with the provisions of the Act.

**7.4** The parties will, six (6) months prior to the nominal expiry date of this Agreement, endeavour to commence negotiations for a replacement Agreement provided that any claim made by any party during this period may not be supported by industrial action.
