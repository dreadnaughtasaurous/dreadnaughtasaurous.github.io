# 9. No Extra Claims

**9.1** This Agreement is reached in full and final settlement of all matters subject to claims by either party and for the life of the Agreement no further claims will be made or supported by the parties covered by the Agreement.

**9.2** Nothing in this clause is intended to be inconsistent with the Act or remove the ability for this Agreement to be varied in accordance with the Act.
