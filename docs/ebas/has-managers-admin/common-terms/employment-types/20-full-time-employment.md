# 20. Full Time Employment



**20.1** A full-time Employee is one who is ready, willing and available to work a full week of 38 hours, or an average of 38 hours as per [clause 22](/ebas/has-managers-admin/common-terms/employment-types/22-part-time-review-of-hours) of Section 2 and [clause 7](/ebas/has-managers-admin/common-terms/preliminary/7-commencement-date-and-period-of-operation) of Section 3 (Hours of Work) at the times and during the hours that are mutually agreed upon, or in the absence of such agreement, as prescribed by the Employer.

**20.2** Subject to the provisions of [clause 22](/ebas/has-managers-admin/common-terms/employment-types/22-part-time-review-of-hours) of Section 2 and [clause 7](/ebas/has-managers-admin/common-terms/preliminary/7-commencement-date-and-period-of-operation) of Section 3 (Hours of Work) a full-time Employee will be paid the full weekly wage for their classification irrespective of the number of hours worked not exceeding 38, or an average of 38 per week.

Plain language terms: full-time, FT.
