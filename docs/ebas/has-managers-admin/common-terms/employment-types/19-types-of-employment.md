# 19. Types Of Employment

**19.1** Employees under this Agreement may be employed in any one of the following employment categories:

- **(a)** full-time employment;
- **(b)** regular part-time employment; or
- **(c)** casual employment.

**19.2** At the time of engagement an Employer will inform each Employee of the terms of their engagement, and in particular whether they are to be full-time, regular part-time or casual.

Plain language terms: employment types, full time, part time, casual, fixed term.
