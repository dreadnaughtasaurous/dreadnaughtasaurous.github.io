# 25. Fixed Term Employment



**25.1** A fixed term Employee is an Employee who is employed for a specified period of time, which period is known at the commencement of the contract, or for a specified task such as a project or replacement of an absent employee.

**25.2** Subject to [clause 25.4](/ebas/has-managers-admin/health-allied-services/hours-of-work/25-overtime), fixed term employment will not be used to fill an ongoing position, or offered through consecutive fixed term employment contracts for work that is otherwise ongoing.

**25.3** Examples of where fixed term employment may be appropriate include:

- **(a)** Graduate positions;
- **(b)** Long term WorkCover replacement;
- **(c)** Special Projects or a body of work with clear parameters determined in advance by the Employer with the intent to deliver a specified outcome within an anticipated timeframe;
- **(d)** Backfill including for extended leave (such as Parental Leave and Long Service Leave), to support flexible working arrangements, temporary assignment or secondment 

    *Note: In this context, backfill to support flexible working arrangements does not refer to the Employee on the flexible working arrangement, but instead to an arrangement to work hours that arise from a flexible work arrangement that includes a temporary reduction in hours.*

**25.4** Where there is a need to continue employment on a fixed term basis beyond the original contract period, further fixed term contracts may be offered only where the arrangement continues to be consistent with [subclause 25.2](/ebas/has-managers-admin/health-allied-services/hours-of-work/25-overtime) and 25.3 above.

**25.5** Where an Employee disputes a matter pertaining to this [Clause 25](/ebas/has-managers-admin/health-allied-services/hours-of-work/25-overtime) - Fixed Term Employment, the Employee should raise the concerns with their immediate supervisor or line manager in the first instance and seek to resolve the issue.

- **(a)** If no resolution can be achieved, the Employee may:
    - **(i)** Seek to have the matter tabled at the Agreement Implementation Committee, or,
    - **(ii)** utilise the Dispute Resolution Procedure set out in [clause 17](/ebas/has-managers-admin/common-terms/consultation-disputes/17-dispute-resolution-procedure) of this Agreement.

Plain language terms: contract, fixed-term.
