# 38. Supported Wage System For Employees With a Disability

**38.1** [Schedule 1B](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/1b-supported-wage-system-for-employees-with-a-disability) defines the conditions that apply to Employees covered by the Agreement who, because of the effects of a disability, are eligible for a supported wage under the terms of the Agreement.
