# 46. Minimum Engagement



**46.1** Each Employee will be paid a minimum of three (3) hours, with the exception of Employees:

**(a)** eligible for payment of overtime in accordance with [clause 25](/ebas/has-managers-admin/common-terms/employment-types/25-fixed-term-employment) of

**(b)** working a period of make up time in accordance with [clause 27](/ebas/allied-health/employment/27-transition-to-retirement) of

**(c)** working a period of telephone recall in accordance with [clause 44.6](/ebas/has-managers-admin/common-terms/allowances/44-on-call-recall) of Section 1 (Recall – Telephone Allowance) and;

**(d)** eligible for payment of overtime in accordance [clause 10](/ebas/has-managers-admin/common-terms/preliminary/10-best-practice-employment-commitment) of Section 3 (Overtime).

**46.2** No Employee will be paid less than the minimum hours of engagement.

Plain language terms: minimum shift, minimum hours.
