# 28. Salary And Allowances Increases

**28.1** The weekly salaries over the life of the Agreement are set out in Schedules 2B and 3B.

**28.2** All allowance rates are provided in [Schedules 2C](/ebas/has-managers-admin/schedules/2c-allowances-health-allied-services) and 3C
