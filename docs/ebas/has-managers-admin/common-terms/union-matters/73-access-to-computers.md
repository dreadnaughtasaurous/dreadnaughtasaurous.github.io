# 73. Access to Computers

**73.1** The parties recognise the increasing dependency on information technology in the workplace, including but not limited to the use of intranets, web based programs and provision of pay slips electronically.

**73.2** An Employer will provide reasonable access to computer and printing facilities for Employees for work-related use, where those Employees are not ordinarily employed in classifications that use computers.

**73.3** Nothing in this clause limits an Employer’s right to implement policies on the appropriate use of information technology in the workplace.
