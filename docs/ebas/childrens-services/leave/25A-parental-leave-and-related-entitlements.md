# 25A. Parental Leave And Related Entitlements

Parental leave and related entitlements are provided for in the [NES](https://www.fwc.gov.au/documents/awards/resources/nes.pdf).

>**Note:** Disputes about requests for extensions to unpaid parental leave may be dealt with under [clause 9](/ebas/childrens-services/consultation-disputes/9-dispute-resolution)---Dispute resolution and/or under section 76B of the [Act](http://www.legislation.gov.au/Series/C2009A00028).

Plain language terms: maternity, paternity, parental.
