**32.2 Definitions**

*   **(a) Affected Employee:** An Employee proposed to be made redundant or who agrees to relocate in excess of a **Reasonable Distance**.
*   **(b) Base Employment Campus:** The campus where the Employee ordinarily starts and finishes work.
*   **(c) Comparable Role:** An ongoing role that is the same occupation/grade, considers the Employee's hours/roster, is within a Reasonable Distance, and accounts for personal circumstances and health/safety.
*   **(d) Consultation:** As defined in [clause 30](/ebas/mspp/employment/30-consultation).
*   **(e) Continuity of Service:** Service is treated as unbroken; personal leave transfer caps do not apply even if other leave is paid out upon termination.
*   **(g) Reasonable Distance:** A distance determined by the original work location, home address, travel capacity, and personal/family circumstances.
*   **(h) Redeployment period:** 13 weeks starting from when the Employer confirms consultation is complete.
*   **(i) Redundancy:** The job is no longer required to be performed by anyone due to operational changes.
*   **(k) Salary maintenance:** A fortnightly allowance representing the difference between the old pay (including higher quals/penalties) and the new role's pay for up to 52 weeks.

---

**32.3 Redeployment**

*   **(a)** Affected Employees will be considered for redeployment during the 13-week period.
*   **(b)** **Written Advice:** Employees must be told the redundancy date, redeployment process details, and their rights/obligations.
*   **(c) Employer Obligations:** Must make every effort to find a **Comparable Role** and give Affected Employees priority for new, substantially similar roles.
*   **(f) Rejecting a Role:** If an Employee rejects a comparable role, they may lose eligibility for a redundancy departure package.
*   **(h) Support:** Includes training (up to 12 weeks supernumerary) and educational support.
*   **(i) No Redeployment:** If redeployment is unlikely or the Employee exits the process, they may accept a redundancy package plus a payment for the remaining redeployment period (up to 13 weeks).



---

**32.4 Support to Affected Employees**

*   **(a) Assistance:** Includes counselling, retraining, application/interview coaching, and paid time off for interviews.
*   **(b) Salary Maintenance:** Provided if the new role is a lower grade, has fewer hours, or reduced penalties.
*   **(d) Leave Preservation:** Accrued Long Service and Annual Leave values are preserved at the pre-redeployment rate.

---

**32.5 Relocation**

*   **(a)** Employers must provide at least 7 days' written notice of a required campus change.
*   **(d) Allowance:** A lump sum up to **$1900.00** may be paid for additional travel costs/expenses for the first 12 months, based on the Employee's written estimate.
*   **(e) Exceptions:** No allowance if the new site was already a potential location under existing employment conditions (unless travel significantly increases).

---

**32.6 Redundancy (Termination)**

*   **(a)** Termination entitlements follow the *Public Sector Industrial Relations Policies 2015*.
*   **(b)** Redundancy is a **last resort**.
*   **(d) Departure Packages:** If no comparable role exists, the Employee (or a volunteer) is offered a Voluntary Departure Package or Targeted Separation Package.

---

**32.7 Severance Pay Exceptions**

*   **(a)** Severance pay does not apply if the Employer secures a comparable role with **another Employer** covered by this Agreement, provided it meets Reasonable Distance and continuity of service requirements.
