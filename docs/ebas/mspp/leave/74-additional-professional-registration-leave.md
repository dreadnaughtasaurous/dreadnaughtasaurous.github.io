# 74. Additional Professional Registration Leave

Psychologists & Pharmacists In addition to the provisions of [clause 73](/ebas/mspp/leave/73-professional-development-leave) – Professional Development Leave above, Pharmacists and Psychologists, both full-time and part-time, shall be entitled to two days of paid Professional Development Leave per annum specifically for the purposes of meeting professional registration requirements.
