# 78. Twelve Month Career Breaks



**a.** Employees may apply for and be granted twelve-month career breaks subject to agreement with the Employer, such agreement not being unreasonably withheld.

**b.** Where an Employee applies for leave pursuant to this clause the Employer shall respond to such applications within four weeks.
