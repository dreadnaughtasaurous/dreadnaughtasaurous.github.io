# 63. Purchased Leave

- **a.** Employees may apply for and be granted Purchased Leave employment arrangements subject to agreement with the Employer, such agreement not being unreasonably withheld.
- **b.** These arrangements are defined as meaning a situation where an Employee takes up to an additional 4 weeks' leave per annum in addition to all other leave entitlements but is paid either 48/52, 49/52, 50/52 or 51/52 of the weekly base rate prescribed by this Agreement for each week during which their employment is subject to these arrangements.
- **c.** Other entitlements will be unaffected by these arrangements.
- **d.** Where an Employee applies for leave pursuant to this clause the Employer shall respond to such request within four weeks.

Plain language terms: 48/52, buying leave, extra leave.
