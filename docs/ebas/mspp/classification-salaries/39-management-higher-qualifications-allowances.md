# 39. Management Higher Qualifications Allowances



Where an employee has a Master of Business Administration, Master of Leadership, Master of Education, Master of Law (including a specialisation), or Master of Public Health Administration higher qualification, which is relevant to their role, they shall be paid the sum of 7.5% of the base rate as defined.

Plain language terms: qualifications allowance, higher qual allowance.
