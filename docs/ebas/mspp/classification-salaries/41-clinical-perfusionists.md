# 41. Clinical Perfusionists

- **a.** For Clinical Perfusionist classification definitions and Higher Qualification Allowances see [Schedule 3](/ebas/mspp/schedules/3-classification-descriptors-and-higher-qualification-allowanc), clause 2.
- **b.** Clinical Perfusionists employed by the Royal Children's Hospital:
    - **(i)** will be covered by Schedule 9 to this Agreement;
    - **(ii)** will not be covered by Schedule 8 to this Agreement, save for clause 7.4 (Consultative Process) of that Schedule; and
    - **(iii)** will not be covered by clauses 55 (Hours of Work), 57 (Rosters), 59 (Overtime), 60 (On Call/Recall), 56 (Shift Work), 80 (Public Holidays), 86 (Travelling Transport and Fares), 91 (Child Care Costs) and 61.10 (Annual Leave Loading) of this Agreement.

Plain language terms: perfusionist.
