# 41. Clinical Perfusionists

- **a.** For Clinical Perfusionist classification definitions and Higher Qualification Allowances see [Schedule 3](/ebas/mspp/schedules/3-classification-descriptors-and-higher-qualification-allowanc), [clause 2](/ebas/allied-health/preliminary/2-arrangement).
- **b.** Clinical Perfusionists employed by the Royal Children's Hospital:
    - **(i)** will be covered by [Schedule 9](/ebas/mspp/schedules/9-royal-children-s-hospital-clinical-perfusionists) to this Agreement;
    - **(ii)** will not be covered by [Schedule 8](/ebas/mspp/schedules/8-royal-women-s-hospital-royal-children-s-hospital-general-emp) to this Agreement, save for clause 7.4 (Consultative Process) of that Schedule; and
    - **(iii)** will not be covered by [clauses 55](/ebas/mspp/hours-of-work/55-hours-of-work) (Hours of Work), 57 (Rosters), 59 (Overtime), 60 (On Call/Recall), 56 (Shift Work), 80 (Public Holidays), 86 (Travelling Transport and Fares), 91 (Child Care Costs) and 61.10 (Annual Leave Loading) of this Agreement.

Plain language terms: perfusionist.
