# 96. No Restraint Of Patients

- **a.** A Psychologist will not be required to physically restrain a patient, except in circumstances where there is an immediate duty of care to maintain safety.
- **b.** Nothing in this clause affects the role a Psychologist may have in participating in de-escalation or other activity other than physical restraint in response to a Code.

Plain language terms: restraint, no restraint.
