# 94. Psychologist Supervisor Training

- **a.** A psychologist who meets the PBA requirements will be approved to undertake supervisor training on request.
- **b.** The Employer will pay reasonable costs, to undertake a PBA approved supervisor training course. Reasonable costs" include (but are not limited to) course fees and time release without loss of pay to undertake the training.
- **c.** Applications will be responded to within 14 days.

Plain language terms: supervisor training.
