# 35. Higher Duties Allowance



Where an Employee is absent from work for any cause and an Employee in a lower class is appointed to assume all the duties and responsibilities of the Employee who is absent for more than five consecutive working days, such Employee shall be entitled to be paid for the period for which they assumed such duties at not less than the minimum rate prescribed for the classification applying to the Employee so relieved.

Plain language terms: acting up, HDA.
