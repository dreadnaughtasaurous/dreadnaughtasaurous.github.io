# 33. Higher Qualifications Allowance



**33.1** Where an Employee has a higher qualification which is relevant to the Employee’s position and duties they shall be paid a weekly allowance calculated on the following percentage of the Class 1 Year 3 rate of pay:

- **(a)** 4% for graduate certificate;
- **(b)** 6.5% for graduate diploma;
- **(c)** 7.5% for masters degree;
- **(d)** 7.5% for MBA; and
- **(e)** 10.0% for PhD or Doctorate of Engineering
