# 45. Rates For Saturdays Sundays

**45.1** All rostered time of ordinary duty performed on a Saturday or on a Sunday shall be paid for at the rate of time and a half.

**45.2** All overtime performed on a Saturday and Sunday will be paid for in accordance with [clauses 46](/ebas/biomedical-engineers/hours-of-work/46-overtime) (Overtime) or 47 (Recall), as applicable.
