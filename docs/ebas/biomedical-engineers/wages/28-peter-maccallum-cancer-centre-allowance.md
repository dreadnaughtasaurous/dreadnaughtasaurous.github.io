# 28. Peter Maccallum Cancer Centre Allowance

**28.1** This clause only applies to Employees employed at Peter MacCallum Cancer Centre.

**28.2** In addition to the wage set out at [Appendix 2](/ebas/biomedical-engineers/appendices/2-wage-rates), an Employee will receive an additional 2.5% of the relevant weekly wage in recognition of the requirement to perform clinical work/radioactive/physicist support.

**28.3** The allowance shall be paid pro rata for part time employees.
