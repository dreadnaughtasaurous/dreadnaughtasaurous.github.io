# 60. Pre Adoption Leave

**60.1** An Employee seeking to adopt a child is entitled to unpaid leave for the purpose of attending any compulsory interviews or examinations as are necessary as part of the adoption procedure.

**60.2** The Employee and the Employer should agree on the length of the unpaid leave. Where agreement cannot be reached, the Employee is entitled to take up to two days unpaid leave.

**60.3** Where paid leave is available to the Employee, the Employer may require the Employee to take such leave instead.

Plain language terms: adoption leave.
