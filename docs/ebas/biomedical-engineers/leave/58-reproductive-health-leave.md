# 58. Reproductive Health Leave

**58.1** In the event the Victorian Government introduces a policy conferring an entitlement to ‘reproductive health leave’, this will apply to Employees but will not form part of this Agreement.

**58.2** Such policy will be incorporated as an entitlement in the enterprise agreement that replaces this Agreement.
