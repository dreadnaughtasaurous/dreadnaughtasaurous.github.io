# 118. Higher Education Recognition Allowance



**118.1** To promote the recruitment and retention of the Lived and Living Experience workforce, all Lived and Living Experience workers shall be entitled to a qualification allowance for any qualification under the Australian Qualifications Framework (or its equivalent) as follows:

**(a)** 4% of the allowance rate for a certificate, diploma or graduate certificate.

**(b)** 6.5% of the allowance rate for a post graduate diploma, degree or double degree

**(c)** 7.5% of the allowance rate for a Masters' degree, doctorate or PhD.
