# 116. Meal Allowance

**116.1** An Employee shall be paid a meal allowance in accordance with [Schedule 2](/ebas/mental-health/schedules/02-salaries-and-allowances):

**116.2** when overtime in excess of one hour is worked after the usual time of ceasing work for the day; or

**116.3** when recalled to duty outside of usual working hours for a period in excess of two hours, and when the time of such recall coincides with or over-runs normal hospital meal time.

**116.4** This clause shall not apply when a meal is supplied at the cost of the Employer.
