# 120. Sole Allowance

An Employee, who is the only person, employed in one of the below listed classifications, shall be paid, in addition to their appropriate rate, an allowance per week at the rate of 5% of the weekly wage of a UG1 grade 1, first year of Experience:

- **(a)** Physiotherapist
- **(b)** Occupational Therapist
- **(c)** Speech Pathologist
- **(d)** Music Therapist
- **(e)** Recreation Therapist
- **(f)** Social Worker
