# 113. Once Off Retention Payment



**113.1** A Once Off Retention Payment of $4,200 will be paid to full time AHP1 Employees at the commencement of the Agreement.

**113.2** The Once Off Payment will be paid over two instalments as follows:

**(a)** FFPPOA commencement of Agreement - $2,100.

**(b)** Next pay period after 113.2(a) - $2,100.

**113.3** The payments will be paid to part time Employees on a pro rata basis.

**113.4** Casual Employees will receive pro rata payments based on average hours over previous 12 months calculated at the time the Agreement commences.
