# 125. Discretionary Backfill for Certain Absences

**125.1 Annual Leave** 

If an Employee is absent on annual leave for a period of 10 days or more and the services provided by the relevant work area would be seriously jeopardised by the absence of the Employee, the Employer should seriously consider providing 100% leave cover for that period.

**125.2 ADOs, conference and study leave** 

An Employer will use its discretion to provide backfill for Conference Leave, Study Leave and ADOs.

**125.3 Consultation** 

For the purposes of this clause, the Employer will consult with the Director of Allied Health, Mental Health (where relevant) or Director of the facility.
