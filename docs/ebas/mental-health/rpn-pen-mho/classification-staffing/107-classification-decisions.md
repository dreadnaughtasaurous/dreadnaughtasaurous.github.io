# 107. Classification Decisions

Classification decisions shall be based upon a documented description of the position such as a duty statement or a position description. Jobs should be evaluated using whole-of-job evaluation:

- **(a)** by comparison of the position description with the narrative descriptions the group and work-level standards such that a comfortable comparison can be made between the nature of work and the general standard of work expected at a particular level; and
- **(b)** by comparison of typical duties (and benchmark positions) to test that the job is recognised to be equal to a majority of positions at one level and better than all positions at a lower level.
