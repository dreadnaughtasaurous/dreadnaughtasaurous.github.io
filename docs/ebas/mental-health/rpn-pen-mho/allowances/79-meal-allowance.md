# 79. Meal Allowance

**79.1 Meal where overtime worked beyond one hour** 

An Employee who works overtime will in the circumstances described in sub-[clause 79.2](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/allowances/79-meal-allowance) receive either:

- **(a)** where an Employer has its own cooking and dining facilities, an adequate meal; or
- **(b)** the relevant meal allowance as set out in [Schedule 2](/ebas/mental-health/schedules/02-salaries-and-allowances).

**79.2 Qualification for meal or payment** 

The entitlement under [subclause 79.1](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/allowances/79-meal-allowance) arises where in addition to a shift an Employee is required to work more than:

- **(a)** one hour beyond the usual finishing hour of work (Monday to Sunday inclusive) in which case the relevant allowance is ‘Allowance A’; or
- **(b)** five hours beyond usual finishing hour of work (Monday to Sunday inclusive) in which case the relevant allowances are ‘Allowance A’ and ‘Allowance B’.

**79.3 Qualification for meal or payment on a rostered day off** 

The entitlement under [subclause 79.1](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/allowances/79-meal-allowance) arises where, on a rostered day off, an Employee is required to work more than:

- **(a)** five hours overtime, in which case the relevant allowance is ’Allowance A’; or
- **(b)** nine hours overtime, in which case the relevant allowances are ‘Allowance A’ and ‘Allowance B’.

**79.4 Where provision does not apply** 

The allowance described at [subclauses 79.2](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/allowances/79-meal-allowance) and 79.3 above is not payable where the Employee:

- **(a)** receives an adequate meal as described at [subclause 79.1(a)](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/allowances/79-meal-allowance); or
- **(b)** could reasonably return home for a meal within the period allowed.

**79.5 Same day payment** 

On request the meal allowance shall be paid on the same day as overtime is worked where practicable.

**79.6 Payments are in addition** 

The payment of a meal allowance under this clause is in addition to any overtime payment.
