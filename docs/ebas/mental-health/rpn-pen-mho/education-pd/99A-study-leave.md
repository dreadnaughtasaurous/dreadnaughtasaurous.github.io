# 99A. Study Leave

*This clause does not apply to casual Employees.*

**99A.1 Entitlement – Registered Psychiatric Nurse (RPN)**

- **(a) Purpose:** Must be used for research or post-graduate studies at a tertiary institution relevant to Mental Health Nursing.
- **(b) Usage:** Can be taken as mutually agreed (e.g., 4 hours/week, 8 hours/fortnight, or 38-hour blocks for residential school).
- **(c) Part-time:** Entitlement is provided on a **pro rata** basis.
- **(d) Accumulation:** Leave does **not** accumulate from year to year.

---

**99A.2 Entitlement – Psychiatric Enrolled Nurse (PEN)**

- **(a) Degree Support:** PENs can access RPN study leave entitlements (99A.1) if undertaking a Bachelor of Nursing.
- **(b) Clinical & Post-Reg Support:** Subject to operations, PENs are eligible for:
    - **(i)** Post-registration mental health modules.
    - **(ii)** Training related to qualification allowances.
    - **(iii)** **Two weeks leave** for clinical placements if enrolled in a course leading to RN registration.

---

**99A.3 Application Process**

Employees must apply in writing as early as possible and include:
- **(i)** Course and institution details.
- **(ii)** Evidence of how the course is relevant to their employment.

**99A.4 Response to Application**

- **(a)** Employers **must not unreasonably refuse** requests.
- **(b)** Written notification of approval/denial must be provided within **seven days**.

---

**99A.5 Mental Health Nursing Scholarships (RPN)**

- **(a) Hours:** Scholarship recipients are entitled to **104 hours** of paid study leave per annum (subject to operations).
- **(b) Scope:** Funding is available for at least 1,000 nurses over the life of the Agreement.

**99A.6 Transition Program (PEN)**

Employers provide a 6–12 month transition program. Specific funding covers a **10-day program** for 550 Enrolled Nurses across the sector.

---

**99A.7 Advanced Diploma in Mental Health Nursing (PEN)**

- **(a) Partnerships:** Employers are encouraged to partner with TAFEs/RTOs for the Advanced Diploma (HLT64121).
- **(b) Goal:** To allow PENs to study while performing clinical hours on-site.
- **(c) Discretion:** Participation in this partnership is at the Employer's discretion.
- **(d) Oversight:** Participating Employers will establish a working party (including Unions and TAFEs) to evaluate the program.

---

**99A.8 Staff Replacement**

All clinical absences for approved study leave **must be backfilled** if the Employee would otherwise have a patient allocation.

---
**Plain language terms:** *exam leave, education leave.*
