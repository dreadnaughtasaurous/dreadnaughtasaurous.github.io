# 93A. Four Clear Days Allowance

Note: for other provisions relating to rosters see [clause 94](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/hours-of-work/94-rosters) (Rosters).

**93A.1** Four Clear Days Allowance

**(a)** Subject to [clause 93A.2(b)](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/hours-of-work/93A-four-clear-days-allowance) below, commencing from the first 28-day roster posted after the date this Agreement commences operation, an Employee is entitled to four clear days in each fortnight of each 28-day roster period free from duty, including on-call/recall work.

**(b)** An Employee who does not receive four clear days off duty as described in [clause 93A.2(a)](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/hours-of-work/93A-four-clear-days-allowance) in a four-week roster period will be paid an allowance of 3.167 hours' ordinary pay (pro rata for part-time Employees) on each applicable occasion.

**93A.2** Alternative

**(a)** An Employer or Union covered by this Agreement may propose alternative arrangements to [clause 93A.1](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/hours-of-work/93A-four-clear-days-allowance) to apply to a ward or unit. The proposal may be implemented where the Employer, the Unions and the majority of affected Employees genuinely agree.

**(b)** Any proposal under [subclause 93A.2(a)](/ebas/mental-health/rpn-pen-mho/hours-of-work/93A-four-clear-days-allowance) will be provided in writing to the affected Employees and Unions.

Plain language terms: clear days, roster, four clear days allowance.
