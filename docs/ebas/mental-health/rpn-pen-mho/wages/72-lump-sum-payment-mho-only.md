# 72. Lump Sum Payment Mho Only

*Note: This clause applies to MHO Employees.*

**72.1** A Once Off Lump Sum Payment of $5,304 will be paid to full time MHO Employees employed at the commencement of the Agreement.

- **(a)** FFPPOA commencement of Agreement - $2,652
- **(b)** Next pay period after 72.1(a) - $2,652.

**72.2** The payments will be paid to part time Employees on a pro rata basis.

**72.3** Casual Employees will receive pro rata payments based on average hours over previous 12 months calculated at the time the Agreement commences.
