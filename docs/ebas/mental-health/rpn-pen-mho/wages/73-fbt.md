# 73. FBT

Employees who use an Employer provided motor vehicle as a result of the performance of their employment duties (eg. crisis assessment treatment team on-call functions) are not to suffer any financial disadvantage, howsoever incurred, as a result of fringe benefits tax (FBT) law.
