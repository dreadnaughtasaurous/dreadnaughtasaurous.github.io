# 181. Staff Appraisal

**181.1** Where a system of staff appraisal does not currently exist at a workplace, the Employer may implement a performance appraisal process and the Employees will participate in that process, provided that:

- **(a)** the Employer first consults at the local level with staff and/or their union or other representative over a framework for the staff appraisal process it is seeking to introduce
- **(b)** the staff appraisal process is not used as a disciplinary tool
- **(c)** the staff appraisal process is intended to allow genuine feedback by both the Employer and Employee; and
- **(d)** the outcomes of the review are documented and confirmed and a written copy of the outcomes in given to the Employee
