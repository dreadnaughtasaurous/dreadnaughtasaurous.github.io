# 153. In Charge Allowances

**153.1** An Employee shall be paid an In-charge Allowance where they are appointed or delegated to exercise control over other Employees, as follows:

| Extent of Control | % Allowance |
|-------------------|-------------|
| In charge of 1 to 9 other Employees | 7% |
| In charge of 10 to 29 other Employees | 10% |
| In charge of 30 or more Employees | 15% |

**153.2** Provided that [clause 153.1](/ebas/mental-health/support-services/preliminary/153-in-charge-allowances) does not apply to the following classifications of Employee:

- **(a)** General Service Supervisor
- **(b)** Food Services Supervisor

**153.3** The percentage In-charge Allowance is calculated on the base rate payable to the Employee pursuant to [Schedule 2](/ebas/mental-health/schedules/02-salaries-and-allowances).

**153.4** The In-Charge Allowance shall be additional to any other allowance to which the Employee is entitled under Section 4.
