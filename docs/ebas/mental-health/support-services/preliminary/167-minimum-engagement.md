# 167. Minimum Engagement

**167.1** Each Employee shall be paid a minimum of three (3) hours per engagement, with the exception of Employees eligible for payment of overtime in accordance with [clause 169](/ebas/mental-health/support-services/preliminary/169-overtime) (Overtime) of Section 4.

**167.2** No Employee shall be paid less than the minimum hours of engagement.
