# 185. Classifications

**185.1** The Employer shall classify all Employees in accordance with the classification structure set out in of this Agreement.

**185.2** The Employer shall notify each Employee in writing upon commencement, of their classification and terms of employment.

**185.3** The Employer shall notify each Employee of any alteration to their classification in writing not later than the operative date of such change.
