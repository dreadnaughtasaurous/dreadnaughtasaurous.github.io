# 201. Minimum Engagement



**201.1** Each Employee shall be paid a minimum of three (3) hours per engagement, with the exception of Employees eligible for payment of overtime in accordance with [clause 203](/ebas/mental-health/management-admin/hours-of-work/203-overtime) (Overtime) of Section 5.

**201.2** No Employee shall be paid less than the minimum hours of engagement.
