# 222. Chief Executive Officers

**222.1** The terms and conditions of employment for Chief Executive Officers (“CEO”) will, in respect of remuneration and related conditions, be those provided for under the Victorian Public Health Entity Executive Remuneration Policy (HEER Policy).).

**222.2** [Clause 222.1](/ebas/mental-health/management-admin/classification-staffing/222-chief-executive-officers) excludes CEOs whose terms and conditions are not covered by a HEER Policy contract.

**222.3** Despite [clause 222.1](/ebas/mental-health/management-admin/classification-staffing/222-chief-executive-officers), any further HEER Policy contract offered by an Employer to an existing Employee whose employment is governed by a HEER Policy contract in existence at the date of certification of this Agreement will not reduce the total remuneration package applicable to that Employee.
