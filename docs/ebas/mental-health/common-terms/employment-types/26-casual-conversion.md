# 26. Casual Conversion

**26.1** A person engaged as a casual employee (as defined under the Act) may, where applicable, request that their employment be converted to full time or part time employment in accordance with the Act. An Employer will consider and respond to any such request in accordance with its obligations under the Act.

Plain language terms: casual to permanent, conversion to permanent.
