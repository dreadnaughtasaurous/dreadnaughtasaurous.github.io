# 6. Application Of Section 3

The terms of Section 3 of this Agreement apply to Employees engaged as Health Professionals in one of the following

- **(a)** Aboriginal Mental Health Clinician, Art Therapist, Child Psychotherapist, Community Development Worker, Counsellor, Exercise Physiologist, Health Information Manager, Lived and Living Experience Workers (LLEW), Music Therapist, Occupational Therapist, Physiotherapist, Play Therapist, Recreational Therapist, Social Worker, Speech Pathologist, Welfare Worker or Youth Worker classifications; and
- **(b)** Pre-Qualified Health Professional student employed by the Employer.
