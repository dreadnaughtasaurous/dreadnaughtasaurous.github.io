# 10. Coverage

**10.1 This Agreement covers:**

- **(a)** the Employers
- **(b)** the Employees
- **(c)** if they are named by the Commission as a party covered by the Agreement, the Health Services Union as a bargaining representative for this Agreement for its members who are Employees classified as RPN, RUSON, Aboriginal Healthcare Worker Trainee, Indigenous Nursing Cadet, PEN, PQEN, MHO, Health Professionals, Pre-Qualified Health Professional Students, Support Services Employees and Management and Administrative Officers; and
- **(d)** if they are named by the Commission as a party covered by the Agreement, the ANMF as a bargaining representative for this Agreement for its members who are Employees classified as RPN, RUSON, Indigenous Nursing Cadet, PEN and PQEN.
