# 5. Application Of Section 2

The terms of Section 2 of this Agreement apply to Employees engaged in one of the following classifications:

- **(a)** Registered Psychiatric Nurse
- **(b)** Psychiatric Enrolled Nurse
- **(c)** Mental Health Officer
- **(d)** Registered Undergraduate Student of Nursing
- **(e)** Pre-qualified Enrolled Nurse; or
- **(f)** Indigenous Nursing Cadet.
