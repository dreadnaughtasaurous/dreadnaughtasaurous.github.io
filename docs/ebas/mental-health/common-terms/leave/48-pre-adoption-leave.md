# 48. Pre Adoption Leave

**48.1** An Employee seeking to adopt a child is entitled to unpaid leave for the purpose of attending any compulsory interviews or examinations as are necessary as part of the adoption procedure.

**48.2** The Employee and the Employer should agree on the length of the unpaid leave.

**48.3** Where agreement cannot be reached, the Employee is entitled to take up to two days unpaid leave.

Plain language terms: adoption leave, adopting a child.
