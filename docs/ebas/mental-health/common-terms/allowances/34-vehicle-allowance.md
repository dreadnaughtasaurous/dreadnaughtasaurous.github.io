# 34. Vehicle Allowance



**34.1** An Employee may only be directed to use their private vehicle, in connection with their duties, where this forms part of their contract of employment or it has been agreed in writing.

**34.2** Where an Employee is required to use their own vehicle, including motor cycle, in connection with their duties, they shall be paid an allowance corresponding with the per kilometre rates as determined from time to time by the Australian Taxation Office \[ATO\] for Work Related Car Expenses (rates per business kilometre)

Plain language terms: car allowance, mileage, kilometre rate, use of private car.
