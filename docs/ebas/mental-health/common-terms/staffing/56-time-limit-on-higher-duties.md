# 56. Time Limit On Higher Duties

**56.1** Where following vacancy advertisement as per [clause 55](/ebas/mental-health/common-terms/staffing/55-recruitment-to-vacant-position), an Employee has been performing higher duties due to a vacancy for a continuous period of 9 months, the employee will be permanently appointed to the role unless the employee requests otherwise in writing.

**56.2** This provision will not apply where the role is subject to a notified change process as per [clause 23](/ebas/mental-health/common-terms/consultation-disputes/23-alternative-dispute-resolution-procedure) and the Dispute Resolution Procedure. Any period between the notification of the change and the implementation of that change will not count for the purpose of 56.1.

Plain language terms: acting up, higher duties allowance, HDA.
