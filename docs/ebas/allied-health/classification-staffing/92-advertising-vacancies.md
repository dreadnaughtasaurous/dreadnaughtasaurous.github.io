# 92. Advertising Vacancies



**92.1** Where a vacancy arises within the Employer, the Employer will advertise the vacant position or available hours, internally in the first instance and then externally if necessary:

- **(a)** where a vacancy will arise at the end of the notice of termination, immediately after giving notice of termination (by either the Employee or Employer); or
- **(b)** where a vacancy will not arise immediately after the end of the notice, such as where the Employee’s role has been backfilled, as soon as practicable.

**92.2** The Employer will advertise all vacancies that arise where the vacancy relates to a position that, but for the vacancy occurring, would have been ongoing.

**92.3** The Employer will appoint someone to a vacant position as soon as practicable.
