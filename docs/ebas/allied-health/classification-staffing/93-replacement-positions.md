# 93. Replacement Positions



**93.1** Subclause 93.2 below applies to the following Health Services only: Alexandra District Health Maryborough District Health Service Bairnsdale Regional Health Service Mildura Base Public Hospital Grampians Health (at those sites that were Omeo District Health formerly Ballarat Health Services and Wimmera Health Care Group) Barwon Health Rochester & Elmore District Health Service Bendigo Health Rural Northwest Health Echuca Regional Health St. Vincent’s Hospital (Melbourne) Limited Central Highlands Rural Health (at those Swan Hill District Health sites that were formerly Hepburn Health Service) Hesse Rural Health Service Tallangatta Health Service Inglewood & Districts Health Service Corryong Health Kerang District Health Western District Health Service Kilmore District Health West Gippsland Healthcare Group Kyabram and District Health Service Yarram and District Health Service

Mallee Track Health and Community Yarrawonga Health Service

**93.2** Every endeavour will be made to appoint to a position that falls vacant on the basis of prolonged leave, within eight (8) weeks of the vacation of the position.
