# 68. Pre Natal Leave

If an Employee is required to attend pre-natal appointments or parenting classes and such appointments or classes are only available or can only be attended during the ordinary rostered shift of an Employee, then on production of satisfactory evidence of attendance at such appointment or class, the Employee may access their carer’s leave credit under this Agreement. The Employee must give the Employer prior notice of the Employee's intention to take such leave.
