# 28A. Patience In Bargaining Payment

*This [clause 28A](/ebas/allied-health/wages/28A-patience-in-bargaining-payment) applies to full-time, part-time, fixed term and casual Employees.*

**28A.1** All Employees who were employed by an Employer upon commencement of this Agreement will be entitled to a once-off ‘Patience in Bargaining’ lump-sum payment calculated as 2.0% of the Employee’s ordinary earnings (which in the case of a casual Employee includes their 25% casual loading) for the period 28 November 2021 to 28 February 2022.

**28A.2** The payment in [subclause 28A.1](/ebas/allied-health/wages/28A-patience-in-bargaining-payment) is payable once this Agreement comes into operation following the approval of this Agreement by the Commission in accordance with the Act.
