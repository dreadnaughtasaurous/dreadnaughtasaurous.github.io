# 40. Sleepover Allowance

**40.1** The sleepover allowance provisions under the 2020 Agreement have ceased to apply.

**40.2** Where an Employer requires an Employee to sleepover on the Employer’s premises, see [subclause 47.7](/ebas/allied-health/hours-of-work/47-hours-of-work) for the applicable payment.
