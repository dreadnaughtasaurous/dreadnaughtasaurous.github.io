# 33. Increases To Allowances



**33.1** The following allowances:

- **(a)** the uniform allowance in [clause 44](/ebas/allied-health/allowances/44-uniform-and-laundry-allowance);
- **(b)** the laundry allowance in [clause 44](/ebas/allied-health/allowances/44-uniform-and-laundry-allowance);
- **(c)** the lead apron allowance in [clause 44A](/ebas/allied-health/allowances/44A-lead-apron-allowance);
- **(d)** the meal allowance in [subclause 36.1](/ebas/allied-health/allowances/36-allowances-related-to-overtime-and-on-call);
- **(e)** the shift allowances in [clause 38](/ebas/allied-health/allowances/38-shift-work-allowance);
- **(f)** CATT on-call allowance in [subclause 54.2](/ebas/allied-health/hours-of-work/54-recall-no-return-to-workplace); and
- **(g)** the change of shift allowance in [clause 39](/ebas/allied-health/allowances/39-change-of-shift-allowance), save that the rate applicable from 1 March 2022 includes the first increase at subclause [clause 28.1](/ebas/allied-health/wages/28-wages-and-wage-increases)(a) (Wage Increases); will be increased in accordance with the wage increases in [subclause 28.1](/ebas/allied-health/wages/28-wages-and-wage-increases).

**33.2** Allowances as amended by this Agreement are set out at [Appendix 3](/ebas/allied-health/appendices/3-allowances-and-top-of-band-payments) of this Agreement.
