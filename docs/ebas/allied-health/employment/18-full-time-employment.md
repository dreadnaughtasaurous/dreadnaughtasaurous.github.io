# 18. Full-Time Employment

An Employee who is required by the Employer to work full-time and is ready, willing and available to work the full number of hours as required by the Employer, will be paid the full weekly wage as prescribed by the Agreement irrespective of the number of hours worked not exceeding 38. [Clause 47](/ebas/allied-health/hours-of-work/47-hours-of-work) (Hours of Work) and [clause 48](/ebas/allied-health/hours-of-work/48-accrued-days-off) (Accrued Days Off) also deal with the manner in which a full-time Employee works their hours.

Plain language terms: full-time, FT.
